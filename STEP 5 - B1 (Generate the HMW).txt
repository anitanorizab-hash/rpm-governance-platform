# B1 — GENERATE THE HMW (HOW MIGHT WE) DOCUMENT

We have completed the Project Discovery and Project Scope Validation phases.

You have already analysed the project folder, project knowledge, business rules, technical rules, AI rules, mentor decisions, data sources, dashboard requirements and architecture decisions.

Now generate the official **How Might We (HMW) Document**.

This document must become the foundation for the Business Requirements Document (BRD).

## Objective

Identify the core business challenges and transform them into innovation opportunities.

Do not propose technical solutions yet.

Focus on business problems and desired outcomes.

---

## Required Sections

### 1. Project Background

Explain the current situation.

Describe why this project is needed.

---

### 2. Current Business Challenges

Identify all existing challenges, including but not limited to:

* KPI monitoring
* Monthly reporting
* Manual Excel process
* Data inconsistency
* Missing information
* Budget monitoring
* Communication between JPN, PPD and Schools
* Alignment with RPM 2026–2035
* Decision making
* Executive reporting

---

### 3. Stakeholders

Identify every stakeholder.

For each stakeholder include:

* Role
* Current challenges
* Desired outcome

---

### 4. Problem Statements

Write clear problem statements.

Example format:

Current Situation

↓

Problem

↓

Impact

---

### 5. How Might We Statements

Generate comprehensive HMW statements.

Examples:

* How might we improve KPI monitoring?
* How might we reduce incomplete KPI information?
* How might we help management identify high-risk KPIs earlier?
* How might we ensure KPI alignment with RPM 2026–2035?
* How might we recommend alternative actions when budget allocation is unavailable?
* How might we improve executive decision support?
* How might we reduce manual reporting effort?
* How might we improve collaboration between JPN, PPD and Schools?

Generate all relevant HMW statements based on the project.

---

### 6. Success Criteria

Explain what success looks like if these problems are solved.

---

### 7. Opportunities

Identify opportunities for:

* AI
* Agentic AI
* Multi-Agent
* Skills Layer
* RAG
* Dashboard
* Executive Copilot
* Budget Intelligence
* Human Review
* Automation

---

### 8. Business Value

Explain the expected value for:

* JPN
* PPD
* Schools
* KPI PIC
* State Management
* Ministry

---

### 9. Constraints

List known business constraints.

Examples:

* Excel only for initial import.
* Monthly update by KPI PIC.
* MOE email only.
* July/October edit restriction.
* Human approval required.
* AI advisory only.

---

### 10. Recommendations

Recommend the most important business priorities before BRD development.

---

## HMW Quality Check

Before finalising, audit your own HMW document.

Check:

* Missing business problem
* Duplicate problem
* Missing stakeholder
* Missing opportunity
* Missing business rule
* Contradictions

Revise automatically if required.

---

## Final Output

At the end, provide:

1. HMW completeness score (%)
2. Confidence score (%)
3. Remaining questions
4. Recommendations before BRD
