# B2 — HMW QUALITY AUDIT

The HMW document has now been generated.

Do not proceed to the BRD yet.

Your task is to perform a professional Business Analysis quality review on the HMW document.

Assume the role of:

* Senior Business Analyst
* Enterprise Architect
* AI Solution Architect
* Government Digital Transformation Consultant

Review the HMW critically.

Do not assume it is correct.

---

## Review Criteria

Evaluate the HMW against the following:

### 1. Business Problem Coverage

Does every important business problem appear?

Check:

* KPI monitoring
* Monthly updates
* Excel dependency
* Missing data
* KPI ownership
* Budget monitoring
* Low Cost High Impact planning
* Notification
* Executive reporting
* RPM alignment
* AI adoption
* Human review
* Governance
* Audit trail

Identify anything missing.

---

### 2. Stakeholder Coverage

Check whether all stakeholders are represented.

Examples:

* JPN Management
* PPD
* School
* KPI PIC
* Sector
* Executive Management
* Administrator

Identify missing stakeholders.

---

### 3. Business Value

Determine whether the HMW clearly explains:

* Why this project is needed.
* Expected benefits.
* Strategic value.
* Operational value.
* AI value.

---

### 4. Innovation Opportunities

Check whether the HMW fully supports:

* Agentic AI
* Multi-Agent Architecture
* Skills Layer
* RAG
* Executive Copilot
* Budget Intelligence
* Low Cost High Impact Matrix
* Knowledge Alignment
* Human-in-the-loop

Identify anything missing.

---

### 5. Alignment Check

Verify that the HMW aligns with:

* Project objectives
* Business rules
* Dashboard requirements
* Monthly workflow
* RPM 2026–2035
* Data architecture
* Knowledge architecture

Highlight inconsistencies.

---

### 6. Readiness for BRD

Determine whether the HMW provides sufficient information to begin writing the BRD.

If not, explain what is missing.

---

## Improvement Task

Automatically revise the HMW based on your findings.

Do not rewrite everything.

Improve only where necessary.

Clearly list:

* Added items
* Removed items
* Updated items

---

## Final Assessment

Provide:

1. HMW completeness score (%)
2. Business coverage score (%)
3. AI readiness score (%)
4. Governance readiness score (%)
5. Overall quality score (%)

Finally answer:

"Is this HMW ready to become the foundation of the BRD?"

Answer only:

YES

or

NO

If NO, explain what still needs to be corrected.
