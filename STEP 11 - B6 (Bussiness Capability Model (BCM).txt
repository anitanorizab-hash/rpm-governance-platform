# B6 — BUSINESS CAPABILITY MODEL (BCM)

The To-Be Business Process has been completed and approved.

Before writing the BRD, identify the business capabilities required by the future system.

Do not describe technical implementation.

Do not describe database tables.

Focus on business capabilities only.

These capabilities will become the foundation for:

* BRD
* System Modules
* AI Agents
* Skills
* APIs
* Dashboard
* User Roles

---

## Objective

Identify every business capability required to successfully operate the Agentic AI Strategic Governance Platform.

---

## Required Sections

### 1. Business Capability Overview

Explain what a Business Capability Model is and why it is important for this project.

---

### 2. Capability Domains

Group capabilities into logical domains.

Suggested domains include (refine if necessary):

1. Governance Management
2. KPI Management
3. Monthly Monitoring
4. Budget & Financial Management
5. Knowledge Management
6. AI Decision Support
7. Reporting & Analytics
8. User & Security Management
9. Notification & Communication
10. System Administration

---

### 3. Capability Details

For each capability provide:

* Capability Name
* Purpose
* Business Value
* Primary Users
* Inputs
* Outputs
* Related Business Rules
* Dependencies

---

### 4. AI Capability Mapping

Identify which capabilities are supported by AI.

Examples:

* KPI Analysis
* Risk Detection
* Budget Intelligence
* Low Cost High Impact Recommendation
* KPI Alignment with RPM 2026–2035
* Executive Copilot
* Chatbot
* Monthly Report Generation

For each AI capability identify:

* Responsible AI Agent(s)
* Supporting Skill(s)
* Human Review Required (Yes/No)

---

### 5. Capability Relationship Diagram

Produce a text-based capability hierarchy.

Example:

Strategic Governance
├── KPI Management
├── Budget Intelligence
├── Knowledge Management
├── Executive Decision Support
├── Reporting
└── Administration

Refine this hierarchy based on the project.

---

### 6. Capability Maturity

For each capability indicate:

* Manual today
* Semi-automated
* Fully automated
* AI-assisted
* Human-in-the-loop

---

### 7. Traceability

For every capability identify links to:

* HMW
* To-Be Business Process
* Future BRD
* Future TRD
* Future AI Architecture

---

### 8. Gap Analysis

Identify any important business capability that is still missing.

Recommend improvements if necessary.

---

## Quality Review

Before finalising:

Check for:

* Missing capabilities
* Duplicate capabilities
* Overlapping responsibilities
* Missing AI support
* Missing governance
* Missing business rules

Improve automatically if required.

---

## Final Output

Provide:

1. Business Capability completeness score (%)
2. Readiness for BRD (%)
3. AI capability coverage (%)
4. Governance coverage (%)

Finally answer:

"Is the Business Capability Model complete enough to begin Business Requirements Definition?"

Answer only:

YES

or

NO

If NO, explain what is still missing.

Do not generate the BRD yet.
