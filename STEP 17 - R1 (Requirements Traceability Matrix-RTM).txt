# R1 — REQUIREMENTS TRACEABILITY MATRIX (RTM)

The Business Requirements Document (BRD) Version 1.0 has been approved and frozen.

Do not modify the BRD.

The BRD is now the official business baseline.

Your next task is to create the Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM).

The RTM will become the bridge between the BRD and the TRD.

Do not generate the TRD yet.

---

## Objective

Create a complete Requirements Traceability Matrix that ensures every approved business requirement can be traced through technical design, implementation and testing.

---

## Traceability Columns

For every approved Business Requirement include:

1. Requirement ID
2. Requirement Name
3. Business Objective
4. Business Rule Reference
5. Business Capability
6. BRD Section
7. Future TRD Section
8. System Module
9. AI Agent (if applicable)
10. Skills Layer (if applicable)
11. Dashboard Component (if applicable)
12. API / Service
13. Database Entity
14. Knowledge Source (if applicable)
15. Test Case Reference
16. Priority
17. Status

## Validation

Review the RTM to ensure:

* Every BRD requirement has at least one technical implementation path.
* No approved requirement is orphaned.
* No technical component exists without a business requirement.
* Every AI Agent supports at least one approved business capability.
* Every Skill is linked to at least one AI Agent.

---

## Final Output

Provide:

1. Total traced requirements.
2. Traceability completeness score (%).
3. Any orphaned requirements.
4. Any orphaned technical components.
5. Readiness to begin the Technical Requirements Document (TRD).
