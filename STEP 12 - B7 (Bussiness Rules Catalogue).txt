# B7 — BUSINESS RULES CATALOGUE

The Business Capability Model has been completed.

Before defining requirements, create a Business Rules Catalogue.

This document will become the single source of truth for all business rules that govern the application.

Do not describe technical implementation.

Do not generate BRD yet.

---

## Objective

Identify, organise and validate every confirmed business rule discovered during Project Discovery, HMW, Business Process Analysis, Business Capability Model and project knowledge.

---

## Required Sections

### 1. Business Rules Overview

Explain the purpose of this catalogue.

---

### 2. Governance Rules

Examples:

* Only authorised users can access the system.
* Human approval is required before official report distribution.
* AI recommendations are advisory only.

---

### 3. User Access Rules

Include:

* Login restricted to `@moe.gov.my` and `@moe-dl.edu.my`.
* Role-based access control.
* Permissions by user role.

---

### 4. KPI Management Rules

Include:

* Initial KPI data imported once from Pelan Taktikal JPN and PPD.
* Monthly updates entered only through the application.
* Every KPI must have a PIC, sector and email.
* Missing mandatory fields trigger warnings.

---

### 5. Amendment Rules

Include:

* KPI Statement, KPI Indicator and Target may only be amended in July and October.
* All amendments require an audit trail.
* Changes must be traceable.

---

### 6. Budget Rules

Include:

* Budget status options.
* Budget Intelligence Agent behaviour.
* Low Cost High Impact Matrix recommendations.
* Human review before implementation.

---

### 7. Knowledge Rules

Include:

* Operational Data and Knowledge Data are separate.
* Knowledge documents feed the RAG pipeline.
* Live knowledge links are supported.
* Chatbot must cite available knowledge where possible.

---

### 8. Reporting Rules

Include:

* Monthly reports.
* Executive summaries.
* Human review workflow.
* Email queue and approval process.

---

### 9. AI Rules

Include:

* Multi-Agent Architecture.
* Skills Layer.
* RAG.
* Executive Copilot.
* AI provider switching using `config.md`.
* AI outputs require human oversight.

---

### 10. Dashboard Rules

Include:

* Dashboard summarises KPIs by Teras 1–7.
* AI-generated executive summary.
* KPI mapping.
* Risk, budget and submission summaries.

---

### 11. Rule Traceability

For each business rule, identify which future documents it affects:

* BRD
* TRD
* System Architecture
* AI Architecture
* Database Design
* Test Cases
