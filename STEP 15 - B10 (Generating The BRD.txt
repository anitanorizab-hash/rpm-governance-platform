# B10 — GENERATE THE BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (BRD)

The BRD outline has been approved.

Now generate the complete Business Requirements Document.

Do NOT generate the entire document in one response.

Generate it section by section.

At the end of each section, stop and wait for my approval before continuing.

The BRD must incorporate all approved information from:

* Project Discovery
* HMW
* As-Is Business Process
* To-Be Business Process
* Business Capability Model
* Business Rules Catalogue
* Master Requirements Catalogue

Do not contradict any previously approved document.

---

## Writing Standards

The BRD should:

* Use professional enterprise language.
* Be suitable for a government software project.
* Be suitable as supporting documentation for academic research.
* Use clear numbering.
* Avoid duplicate information.
* Reference previously approved artefacts where appropriate.

---
