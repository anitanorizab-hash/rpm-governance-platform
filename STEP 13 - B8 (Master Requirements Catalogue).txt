# B8 — MASTER REQUIREMENTS CATALOGUE

The Business Rules Catalogue has been completed.

Before Stakeholder Analysis and BRD development, create a Master Requirements Catalogue.

This catalogue will become the master source for all project requirements.

Do not write the BRD yet.

Do not write the TRD yet.

---

## Objective

Identify, classify and organise every project requirement discovered so far.

Each requirement must have a unique Requirement ID.

---

Classify requirements into:

### 1. Business Requirements

Examples:

* Monthly KPI monitoring
* Executive reporting
* KPI governance
* Budget monitoring

### 7. Reporting Requirements

---

## For Every Requirement

Provide:

* Requirement ID
* Requirement Name
* Description
* Category
* Priority

  * Must Have
  * Should Have
  * Could Have
  * Future Enhancement
* Source
  (Business Rule, Mentor Decision, HMW, etc.)
* Related Capability
* Related Business Rule
* Related Future Module

---

## Requirement Validation

Review the catalogue for:

* Missing requirements
* Duplicate requirements
* Conflicting requirements
* Low-priority items that can be deferred

Automatically improve where necessary.

---

## Final Output

Provide:

1. Total requirements identified.
2. Requirements by category.
3. MoSCoW prioritisation summary.
4. Readiness for Stakeholder Analysis.
